Cars are Color

Automotive coatings are an integral part of CCI's color merchandising market. Our highly specialized automotive products celebrate the most popular color choices from the past, the present, and the future.

We have designed unique equipment, one-of-a-kind machinery, and color solutions for the distinct needs of our automotive customers. The complex makeup of automotive color is replicated in the color standards and products produced for such high-performance applications.

CCI's extensive automotive work exhibits the highest level of accuracy and a variety of highly specialized techniques and materials that CCI is known for.

Automotive Color Evolution

Driving the Future of Automotive Color

We are proud to say that a large component of our main business comes from the automotive color industry. Both OEM and Refinish companies use our color tools to help car buyers around the world decide on a new car purchase, or a new paint for a car they own. As personal car lovers, we are very proud to play a role in the automotive color market.

Over the past decade, there have been significant changes to both the automotive industry. There have also been many many changes in the Automotive industry and understandably, there have also been great changes in the materials used, process development, greener efforts, and methods of paint application.

Hand spraying was previously used and has evolved into 'robotic spraying'. Many of these factors have made it difficult to replicate an exact match for an automotive paint, but CCI has strategically changed with its customers to continue serving the automotive industry. Products manufactured for automotive color sampling highlight the unrivaled quality and precision that CCI is known for. We are proud that our customers in both Automotive OEM and Refinish rely on CCI for their integral color needs with confidence.
