GREEN PRACTICES

Color Communications, Inc. introduced environmentally responsible practices long before they were widely acknowledged to be important factors.

We are committed to ensuring that the quality of life in color remains in tact with our Green production processes and materials.

Preserving Nature's Vivid Colors

Environmentally Safe Practices in Color Production

Eco-Friendly Color

  • Specialized WaterColors® technique that is used daily in regular production;eco-friendly and odor-free without compromising color quality.
  • FSC® Certified papers and vegetable oil based ink & aqueous clear coat.
  • We have an extensive factory recycling and energy efficiency program in place for all facilities.
  • Our paints & production materials generate minimal VOC fumes.

Eco-Friendly Production for the Most Accurate Color

Color with a Conscience...

Large companies with the capacity to run greener processes have an obligation to our environment.

Today we are constantly working to further eliminate harmful materials and techniques to better serve the global market with safe and accurate color marketing systems.
